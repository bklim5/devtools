# Phase 22.1 Plan 03: License-Pane Redesign Summary

Redesigned the Settings ▸ License pane and the shared activation surface to the
2026-06-15 walkthrough direction: a confident pitch hero, semantic color system,
and calm-but-distinct managed-state banners — while reversing D-20 to show in-app
pricing ($9 lifetime). No activation logic changed (the Plan-02 shared surface is
intact); this is presentation + two new token triads + a refresh-stickiness fix.

## What Shipped

- **Design tokens (`8c8b6883`):** new `--color-warn` (#fbbf24) amber triad and
  `--color-ok` (#34d399) green triad in `index.css`, each a 15% `-soft` fill + 75%
  `-line` via `color-mix`, mirroring the accent triad exactly. Dark-theme only.
- **Pitch redesign (`1e2178a7`, `cdc9c994`):** accent radial glow over the panel, a
  borderless accent medallion above a 24px "Thank you for using TinkerDev ❤️" hero,
  a 3-row feature list (Custom themes / Reorder & pin / Fund what's next), a $9
  price block ("once · lifetime license"), a claims footer, and a Key-icon prefix
  inside the license-key input. **D-20 reversed** — pricing is now in-app.
- **Attention card (`ec14c64e`, `cdc9c994`):** problem/refreshNeeded render a
  3-column amber banner [AlertTriangle | content + UNVERIFIED pill | secondary
  Refresh] over a separator + the inline "Activate a license" form (no pitch — a
  paying customer never sees the upsell). "Lost your key? Check your purchase email."
- **Licensed banner + Deactivate (`fc9c5cd0`, `d1fdbd41`):** green success banner
  (green dot + "Licensed" + PRO pill + secondary Refresh) over a neutral
  Licensee/masked-key detail table; the confirm-first Deactivate trigger is
  left-aligned and reveals a full-width red (`--color-bad`) confirm card
  [icon | content | Cancel + destructive Deactivate], focus → confirm on reveal.
- **Refresh stickiness (`c06baae3`):** `carryForwardKeyEmail` in `licenseUi.ts`
  keeps maskedKey/email populated across a keychain-free refresh when the Pro-active
  state is unchanged, so the License key never blanks on a footer/panel/startup refresh.
- **Heading-order fix (`20943f78` → `19f93cb4`):** the visible pane title is `<h3>`
  (one level under the dialog's `<h2>`), not `<h1>` — no heading-order inversion.
- **Chore (`588a9e0d`):** gitignore `*.tsbuildinfo` (tsc incremental artifacts).
